Shipping

There are no shipping charges for tickets which have been booked online. You will be able to download the tickets once the booking has been completed. They need to be printed and carried along.

The shipping charges for “Publications” products shipped to an address within Germany are EUR 6.50 (parcel up to 5 kg). The shipping charges for delivery abroad will be calculated according to cost.
